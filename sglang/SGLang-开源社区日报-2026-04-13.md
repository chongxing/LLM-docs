### #21864 - [lora] Fix partial MoE rank loading, VL lm_head, strict loading, deepseek on-demand
- **链接**: https://github.com/sgl-project/sglang/pull/21864
- **作者**: @klshuster
- **状态**: ✅ MERGED (2026-04-12 23:25 UTC)
- **标签**: high priority, lora
- **描述**: 修复多个LoRA加载bug，影响正确性和可用性

**修复内容**:
1. **Partial MoE rank loading** - 当LoRA adapter的rank少于max_lora_rank时，A-buffer堆叠组件放置位置错误
2. **VL model lm_head LoRA** - vision-language模型的`should_apply_lora`模式跳过embed_tokens/lm_head模块
3. **Strict LoRA loading** - 添加`--lora-strict-loading`标志，预验证adapter权重名称
4. **DeepSeek on-demand loading** - 修复`"all"`目标模块sentinel过早展开的问题
